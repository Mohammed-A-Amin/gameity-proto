Gamers Outreach is an organization that was founded in 2007 and has provided equipment, technology, and software to help kids cope with treatment inside hospitals. Undergoing medical treatment can often be a scary and isolating experience, especially for young people. Their focus is to support kids and teens throughout the healing process as they undergo treatment in hospitals. They raise money to equip nurses and child life specialists with the means to make activities and technology accessible. Ultimately, their goal is to create sustainable experiences that produce joy and minimize trauma for patients.
