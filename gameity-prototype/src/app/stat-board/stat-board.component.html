VALORANT

VALORANT has a ranking system which is separated into eight tiers. Starting with Iron and ending with Radiant. All tiers except Radiant and Immortal have three sub-ranks within them, with rank one the lowest and rank three the highest. All of these ranks have different playstyles aswell as how long they play the game for.
