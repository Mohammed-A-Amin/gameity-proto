Games that Support Charities!

    VALORANT® is a 5v5 tactical shooter created by developer Riot Games. This game hosts many tournaments in which pros can play in order to fundraise as much money as they can for charity. You yourself can participate in one of these tournaments by progressively ranking the VALORANT leaderboard in the Competetive scene and be able to give back to your community by playing this game. Some tournaments such as the VALORANT Charity Invitational hosted by the Pittsburgh Knights have been successful in bringing together the community.

    Super Smash Bros. is a fighting game developed by Nintendo®. SSB has long had a tight-knit community, and a recent charity endeavor put that to work for a good cause. The #SmashTheRecord event began just last year, when player Jason “Gahtzu” Diehl worked with two Florida gaming groups to raise money for charity by breaking the world record for “Longest Video Games Marathon Playing a Fighting Game.” That 72-hour marathon netted $14,000 for St. Jude’s Children’s Research Hospital. If you're good at Smash Bros, why not use this opportunity to play for charity?

    Fortnite is a 100 player Battle Royale game developed by Epic Games.Fortnite has many opportunities to play for the cause of charity. Many different organizations such as Cure Rare Disease ®.If you think you're good enough to compete in these tournaments to gain money yourself and also for charity, why not give it a go? Through lots of practice, Fortnite gamers who are passionate about helping their community may be able to sign up for these kinds of tournaments. Gamers Without Border hosted a 50-team Fortnite: Battle Royale charity tournament from May 19-21 with a US$2 million prize pool that was donated to coronavirus relief charity foundations.

    Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. With the level of creativity you can do for anything in this game, comes with more opportunities. In minecraft, you can make builds, join PVP tournaments, buy cosmetics from multiplayer servers, and much more for the cause of supporting charities that matter. Minecraft Ultimate, which used to be a charity tournament for Minecraft gamers, has played 2 seasons and managed to raise more than $75,000 for charities that the participating teams choose to play for.

    Mario Kart is a series of go-kart-style racing video games developed and published by Nintendo. Many people are good at racing at Mario Kart, and if you think you have what it takes to challenge them during a charity race, then go for it! You'll have the chance to show off your cool drifting skills and end up at the top! The National Esports Association hosted 4 weeks of virtual Mario Kart 8 Deluxe tournaments as a benefit for Extra Life. They ended up raising over $1,000 from that tournament!
