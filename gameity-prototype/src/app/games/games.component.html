Gaming For Everyone

There are various types of games that you can play in order to contribute to charity. There's always something for everyone! Below, you may find your favorite game that you play regularly that actually helps out in charity. If you have any more suggestions about games to add to Gameity, feel free to contact us!

Valorant
VALORANT® is a 5v5 tactical shooter created by developer Riot Games. This game hosts many tournaments in which pros can play in order to fundraise as much money as they can for charity. You yourself can participate in one of these tournaments by progressively ranking the VALORANT leaderboard in the Competetive scene and be able to give back to your community by playing this game.
Platforms
Microsoft Windows
Age Requirement
16
Type
Multiplayer Tactical First-Person Hero Shooter
SuperSmash Bros Ultimate
Super Smash Bros. is a fighting game developed by Nintendo®. SSB has long had a tight-knit community, and a recent charity endeavor put that to work for a good cause. The #SmashTheRecord event began just last year, when player Jason “Gahtzu” Diehl worked with two Florida gaming groups to raise money for charity by breaking the world record for “Longest Video Games Marathon Playing a Fighting Game.” That 72-hour marathon netted $14,000 for St. Jude’s Children’s Research Hospital. If you're good at Smash Bros, why not use this opportunity to play for charity?
Platforms
Nintendo Switch
Age Requirement
13
Type
Fighting game, Cooperative game theory, Crossover
Fortnite
Fortnite is a 100 player Battle Royale game developed by Epic Games.Fortnite has many opportunities to play for the cause of charity. Many different organizations such as Cure Rare Disease ®.If you think you're good enough to compete in these tournaments to gain money yourself and also for charity, why not give it a go? Through lots of practice, Fortnite gamers who are passionate about helping their community may be able to sign up for these kinds of tournaments.
Platforms
Play Station, Xbox, Switch, PC/MAC, Mobile
Age Requirement
12
Type
Battle Royale
Minecraft
Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang. With the level of creativity you can do for anything in this game, comes with more opportunities. In minecraft, you can make builds, join PVP tournaments, buy cosmetics from multiplayer servers, and much more for the cause of supporting charities that matter.

Age Requirement
7
Type

Mario Kart
Mario Kart is a series of go-kart-style racing video games developed and published by Nintendo. Many people are good at racing at Mario Kart, and if you think you have what it takes to challenge them during a charity race, then go for it! You'll have the chance to show off your cool drifting skills and end up at the top!

COD Warzone
Call of Duty Warzone is a 150 player Battle Royale game made by Activision Blizzard. The Battle Royale mode is similar to other titles in the genre where players compete in a continuously shrinking map to be the last player remaining. Players parachute onto a large game map, where they encounter other players. As the game progresses and players are eliminated, the playable area shrinks forcing the remaining players into tighter spaces. With the ability to host private matches in Warzone, people are able to host private tournaments for charity in this game. Tournaments such as the TXT Warzone tournament has brung together the community to donate to charity while having fun in Warzone.
Platforms
Play Station, Xbox, PC
Age Requirement
18
Type
First Person Shooter, Battle Royal Game
